North Bay Lighting was founded in San Rafael by Mr. Ed in 1980. With an understanding of our need for the electric future, Ed built NBL into a thriving business whilst serving Marin county.

In addition to electrical products for commercial work, we serve a wide variety of specialty lighting bulbs and features.
